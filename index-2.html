ABOUT

Initiatives Foundation: An amazing Platform to learn and showcase yourself

Executive

Corona virus outbreak has started in the country since the beginning of 2020. Since then, it is still going on, on the contrary, it seems to be increasing day by day. And as more and more people are being housebound by the Corona outbreak, the number of small entrepreneurs leaving the offline world and into the online world is increasing. Someone is making a living by freelancing in the pursuit of life! Someone is doing human service with the help of different foundations. During the corona outbreak, various clubs and foundations have been set up online, some of which are standing by the helpless, while others are becoming a time to spend time with everyone from children to young people stumbling in the social world. One such community is the Initiatives Foundation. Formerly they were the 'Ignite Learn-Study Group'.

‘Initiatives Foundation’ started its journey on 9th March, 2020. The previous name of this community was ‘Study Group’ and the main purpose was only to provide people new facts about Study and Education. But after launching their first event, Initiatives Foundation broadened their way of thinking. Now, the broader plan includes all types of topics related to Education and Skill Development. Through these steps, Initiatives Foundation wants to provide this sector the spotlight it deserves and provide a platform for their fans.

Initiatives Foundation has an Executive Committee of 10 Core panels and about 100+ Official Coordinators who are from different renowned institutions of Bangladesh and who seek the present rather than the future. This organization arranged the very first online superhero event of Bangladesh and the very first online Anime event of Bangladesh.

Our Motto is "Learn Together, Teach Together " We, here, try to learn and teach the educational content and ECA with fun and joy. Here we contribute songs, dances and other cultural activities of our beloved audiences in our talent showcase group: 'Talent Enthusiasts : Showcase your talent with Ignite Learn'. In our another group, 'Ignite Learn Community', we share various educational content regarding every type of knowledge upto culture and heritage. And we also have a small fun community named "Chiriakhana Station" and news sharing group named "Contagious Intelligence" We routinely share facts and anyone interested can find his way upto post there. Finally, we have one group for your Talent Showcase, another group for education and research. We are here to give you the educational and Cultural facilities you'ld want! Stay with The Initiatives Foundation
